About Me

A little about who I am

I’m a dedicated IT student with a deep interest in cloud, networking, and technical support. I’ve built my foundational knowledge through online courses and training, and I continue to expand my expertise by configuring routers, switches, and firewalls, as well as exploring hybrid cloud deployments on AWS. I believe good infrastructure should be invisible: fast, reliable, and secure.

In addition to my studies, I am honored to serve as an AWS User Group BuildHERS+ Ambassador — a role that allows me to connect with peers, share knowledge, and advocate for inclusivity in technology. I thrive in collaborative environments where ideas are exchanged freely.
